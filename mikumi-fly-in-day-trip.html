Safari Overview

Discover the wild side of Tanzania with a one-day safari adventure to Mikumi National Park, perfect for travelers based in Zanzibar who want a taste of the African savannah without the need for a long overland journey. This fly-in, fly-out day trip offers a seamless experience that combines convenience, comfort, and incredible wildlife viewing.

Mikumi National Park is part of the greater Selous ecosystem and is known for its scenic landscapes and abundant wildlife, making it a great safari destination for first-time visitors or those with limited time.

Mikumi National Park

Your journey begins with a hotel transfer in Zanzibar and a scenic domestic flight to Mikumi, one of Tanzania's most accessible and wildlife-rich national parks. Upon landing, you'll be welcomed by a professional safari guide and begin your full-day game drive in an open 4x4 vehicle. The Park is home to an impressive variety of wildlife, including elephants, lions, zebras, giraffes, wildebeests, buffaloes, impalas, and numerous bird species. The open plains of Mikumi provide excellent visibility, making it easy to spot animals in their natural habitat.

During the safari, you'll stop for a picnic lunch inside the park, giving you a chance to relax and soak in the beautiful surroundings. In the late afternoon, you'll return to the airstrip for your flight back to Zanzibar, followed by a transfer back to your hotel.

Meals: Picnic Lunch
