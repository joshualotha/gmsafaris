Safari Overview

Embark on an unforgettable Tanzania Safari Adventure, where you'll explore the country's most iconic national parks, including Tarangire National Park, Serengeti National Park, the Ngorongoro Crater, and Lake Manyara National Park. Experience game drives through vast wildlife-rich landscapes, get a glimpse into the lives of the Maasai people (optional), and witness the beauty of diverse ecosystems. This 5-day journey offers the perfect blend of wildlife and cultural encounters, with an option to start or end your tour in Zanzibar or Arusha.

Detailed Itinerary

1

Travel to Tarangire National Park

Arusha to Tarangire NP

Your adventure begins with a pick-up from your hotel in Arusha, driving towards Tarangire National Park. Expect to enter the park around 10 am. Your first game drive (approx. 3 hours) explores Tarangire's diverse wildlife. Enjoy a delicious lunch box under an acacia tree or at a picnic site. Continue your game drive until about 4-5 pm. Exit the park and drive to Farm of Dreams for dinner and overnight.

Meals: Lunch Box, Dinner

Accommodation: Farm of Dreams

2

Drive to Serengeti via Ngorongoro

Farm of Dreams to Serengeti NP (Seronera)

After breakfast at Farm of Dreams, depart early for Serengeti National Park. Pass through the Ngorongoro Conservation Area, observing human-wildlife harmony. Optional: visit a Maasai Boma (extra cost) or Olduvai Gorge (extra cost). Lunch at Nabi Gate before entering Serengeti. Continue towards Seronera for dinner and overnight in a tented camp.

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

Accommodation: Moyo Tented Camp / Tukaone Camp

3

Full-Day Serengeti Safari

Serengeti National Park

Breakfast at the lodge/camp. Full day dedicated to game drives across Serengeti's vast plains. Explore diverse wildlife, Big Five, and possibly Great Migration (seasonal). Lunch box provided. Optional: hot air balloon ride (extra cost). Return to lodge/camp in late afternoon.

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch Box, Dinner

Accommodation: Moyo Tented Camp / Tukaone Camp

4

Serengeti to Ngorongoro Crater

Serengeti NP to Ngorongoro Crater Rim

After breakfast, pack and drive towards Ngorongoro Crater through Ngorongoro Highlands. Descend into the crater floor for a game drive. Spot diverse wildlife, including black rhino. Visit Ngoitokitoki Spring for a lunch break. Ascend the crater and head to Farm of Dreams for dinner and overnight.

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch Box, Dinner

Accommodation: Farm of Dreams

5

Lake Manyara National Park & Return to Arusha/Zanzibar

Lake Manyara NP, Arusha/Zanzibar

Following breakfast, drive to Lake Manyara National Park, entering around 10 am. Enjoy a 3-hour game drive exploring birdlife, forests, biodiversity, and famous tree-climbing lions. Lunch box at a picnic site by the hot spring (early or late lunch option). Exit park and drive back to Arusha. Option to fly to Zanzibar or continue stay in Arusha.

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch Box

Important Notes

  • Accommodation based on choice of tented lodges or camps.
  • Optional activities (Maasai Boma, Olduvai Gorge, Hot Air Balloon) are at additional cost.
  • Safari can end with drop-off in Arusha or flight to Zanzibar.
  • International and domestic flights (unless specified for Zanzibar return) are not included.
