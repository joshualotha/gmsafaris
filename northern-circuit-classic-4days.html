Safari Overview

Embark on a four-day adventure that takes you through Tanzania's most iconic and breathtaking wildlife destinations. From the towering elephants of Tarangire National Park to the wide-open plains of the Serengeti, and the unparalleled beauty of the Ngorongoro Crater, this safari is the perfect way to experience Tanzania's natural wonders. Starting from Zanzibar or Arusha, this journey offers thrilling game drives, luxury accommodations, and a deep dive into the heart of Africa's wildlife.

Detailed Itinerary

1

Zanzibar/Arusha to Tarangire National Park

Tarangire National Park

Your adventure begins with a morning flight from Zanzibar to Arusha (if applicable), where you will be greeted by your guide upon arrival. After a brief meet and greet, you will embark on a scenic drive to Tarangire National Park, famous for its large elephant herds and striking baobab trees. Tarangire is a haven for wildlife, offering excellent opportunities to spot not only elephants but also lions, zebras, giraffes, and an array of bird species. You'll enjoy your first game drive in the park, soaking in the beauty of this diverse ecosystem. After your exciting day, you'll head to your comfortable lodge or tented camp for the night, where dinner will be served and you can relax before your next adventure.

Meals: Lunch, Dinner

Accommodation: Mawemawe Manyara Lodge/ Farm of Dreams/Sound of Silence (or similar)

2

Tarangire to Serengeti National Park

Serengeti National Park

After a delicious breakfast, you'll depart for the iconic Serengeti National Park, one of the most famous wildlife reserves in the world. The Serengeti is renowned for its vast plains and abundant wildlife, and your drive through the park will offer numerous opportunities to spot animals such as lions, leopards, cheetahs, elephants, and wildebeests. Upon arrival, check in at your Serengeti accommodation before enjoying an afternoon game drive in the park. The Serengeti is especially well-known for its incredible seasonal migrations, making this park an unforgettable experience. As the sun sets, you'll return to your lodge or camp for dinner and overnight.

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

Accommodation: Moyo Tented Camp/ Tukaone Tented Camp (or similar)

3

Full Day Game Drive in Serengeti National Park

Serengeti National Park

Day 3 is dedicated to exploring the Serengeti's famous wildlife and stunning landscapes. After an early breakfast, you'll embark on a full-day game drive with your expert guide, taking you deep into the park's different areas to maximize your chances of spotting a wide range of animals. The Serengeti is home to the Big Five—lion, leopard, elephant, buffalo, and rhino—and you may also encounter giraffes, gazelles, zebras, and various species of birds. A packed lunch will be provided during the game drive, and you'll have a chance to relax and enjoy the landscape before continuing your adventure. After a full day of wildlife exploration, you'll return to your lodge or camp for the night.

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

Accommodation: Moyo Tented Camp/ Tukaone Tented Camp (or similar)

4

Serengeti to Ngorongoro Crater, then Return to Arusha/Zanzibar

Ngorongoro Crater, Arusha

On your final day, you will depart from the Serengeti early in the morning, heading to the stunning Ngorongoro Crater, one of the most unique and biodiverse places on Earth. Upon arrival, you will descend into the crater, a vast volcanic caldera teeming with wildlife. This UNESCO World Heritage site is home to the Big Five and many other species, including flamingos, hippos, and wildebeests. Enjoy a game drive on the crater floor, where you'll witness a wide variety of animals in their natural habitat. After your game drive and a picnic lunch in the crater, you will begin your journey back to hotel in Arusha or Kilimanjaro Airport. In the evening, you will board a flight back to Zanzibar (if applicable), concluding your unforgettable safari adventure.

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch
