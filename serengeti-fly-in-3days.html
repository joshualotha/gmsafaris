Safari Overview

Experience the magic of the Serengeti with this unforgettable 3-day fly-in safari from Zanzibar/Arusha. Designed for travelers who want to maximize their time in the wild without the long drive, this adventure takes you straight to the heart of Tanzania's most iconic landscapes. You'll explore the vast plains of the Serengeti, track the Big Five, and descend into the breathtaking Ngorongoro Crater all while enjoying comfortable accommodation at the charming Moyo Tented Camp.

This tour is perfect for wildlife lovers, couples, families, or honeymooners looking for an authentic safari experience with a touch of luxury and convenience.

Detailed Itinerary

1

Zanzibar – Fly to Serengeti National Park

Central Serengeti

Your adventure begins with a scenic flight from Zanzibar to the Seronera Airstrip, located in the heart of the Serengeti. Upon arrival, your safari guide will greet you for a half-day game drive through the central Serengeti, one of the most wildlife-rich areas in Africa. Look out for lions, elephants, giraffes, and more roaming the endless plains. As the sun sets, head to your camp for dinner and overnight at Moyo Tented Camp, offering a cozy, authentic bush experience in a beautiful setting.

Meals: Lunch, Dinner

Accommodation: Moyo Tented Camp

2

Full-Day Game Drive in Serengeti

Serengeti National Park

After breakfast, set off for a full day of exploration in the Serengeti National Park. This is your chance to witness the true wonders of the African savannah whether it's herds of zebras and wildebeest on the move, a leopard resting in a tree, or a pride of lions stalking prey. With your professional guide, you'll follow animal tracks, visit waterholes, and enjoy a packed lunch in the wild. Return to camp in the evening to relax under the stars.

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

Accommodation: Moyo Tented Camp

3

Serengeti – Ngorongoro Crater – Arusha – Zanzibar

Ngorongoro Crater, Arusha

After an early breakfast, depart for a scenic drive to the Ngorongoro Conservation Area. Descend into the world-famous Ngorongoro Crater, a UNESCO World Heritage Site and home to one of the highest densities of wildlife in Africa. Enjoy a game drive on the crater floor with chances to see rhinos, hippos, and large predators in a dramatic setting. After the safari, continue by road to hotel in Arusha or Kilimanjaro Airport for your return flight to Zanzibar.

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch

Important Notes

  • This tour can start from Zanzibar or Arusha.
  • Accommodation is at the authentic Moyo Tented Camp.
  • Flights (International/Domestic to start point) are NOT included unless specified. Return flight to Zanzibar from Arusha/JRO is part of Day 3.
  • Recommended tipping: $10-$15 per person per day for the guide.
  • Travel insurance is mandatory.
