Safari Overview

This 7-day semi-luxury safari offers an immersive experience of Tanzania's northern circuit, combining wildlife exploration with cultural encounters. Traverse diverse ecosystems, from the lush landscapes of Lake Manyara to the vast plains of the Serengeti and the rich biodiversity of Ngorongoro Crater. Engage with indigenous tribes like the Hadzabe and Datoga, gaining insight into their traditional lifestyles. The tour provides a balanced mix of adventure, relaxation, and cultural enrichment, staying in comfortable semi-luxury lodges and tented camps.

Detailed Itinerary

1

Arrival in Arusha & Lake Manyara National Park

Arusha, Lake Manyara NP, Mto wa Mbu

Upon arrival at Kilimanjaro International Airport (JRO), you'll be greeted and transferred to Lake Manyara National Park. Enjoy an afternoon game drive spotting diverse wildlife. Picnic lunch in the park. In the evening, experience a cultural tour in Mto wa Mbu village.

Meals: Lunch, Dinner

Accommodation: Mawemawe Manyara Lodge (or similar semi-luxury)

2

Serengeti National Park via Ngorongoro Crater

Ngorongoro Conservation Area, Serengeti NP (Central)

After breakfast, depart for Serengeti National Park, passing through Ngorongoro Conservation Area. Enjoy panoramic views and a brief game drive en route. Continue to Naabi Hill Gate for entry into Serengeti. Picnic lunch, then proceed to central Serengeti for an afternoon game drive.

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

Accommodation: Moyo Tented Camp (or similar semi-luxury)

3

Full Day in Serengeti National Park

Serengeti National Park (Central)

Spend a full day exploring the vast Serengeti plains. Morning and afternoon game drives searching for the Big Five and other wildlife. Packed lunch during the day. Return to your lodge/camp for relaxation and overnight stay.

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

Accommodation: Moyo Tented Camp (or similar semi-luxury)

4

Serengeti to Northern Serengeti

Serengeti National Park (Northern)

Rise early for a game drive towards the northern Serengeti, known for unique landscapes and wildlife. Enjoy a full-day game drive with a packed lunch. Optional: Hot air balloon ride (additional cost applies).

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

Accommodation: Moyo Tented Camp (or similar semi-luxury, northern location if specified by camp options)

5

Serengeti to Ngorongoro Crater & Lake Eyasi

Ngorongoro Crater, Lake Eyasi

After breakfast, depart Serengeti and head towards Ngorongoro Crater. Descend into the crater floor for a half-day game drive. Picnic lunch at Ngotokitoki Picnic Site. Ascend the crater and transfer to Lake Eyasi Safari Lodge for dinner and overnight.

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

Accommodation: Lake Eyasi Safari Lodge (or similar semi-luxury)

6

Visit to Hadzabe and Datoga Tribes, then Mto wa Mbu

Lake Eyasi, Mto wa Mbu

Early morning excursion to join the Hadzabe tribe on a traditional hunting expedition. Learn about their hunter-gatherer lifestyle. Later, visit the Datoga boma. Return to lodge for lunch. In the afternoon, drive to Mto wa Mbu for a cultural tourism experience.

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

Accommodation: Mawemawe Manyara Lodge (or similar semi-luxury)

7

Tarangire National Park & Return to Arusha

Tarangire NP, Arusha

After breakfast, depart for Tarangire National Park. Enjoy a full day of game drives with a picnic lunch in the park. In the late afternoon, exit the park and drive back to Arusha. Conclude your safari with a farewell dinner and overnight stay in Arusha (or transfer to JRO for departure).

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner (Farewell)

Accommodation: (Overnight in Arusha or departure)
