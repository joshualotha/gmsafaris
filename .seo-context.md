## Target Audience
- Primary user persona: Affluent international traveler aged 25–65, planning a once-in-a-lifetime safari or Tanzania holiday. They research extensively (2–6 months before booking), compare multiple operators, read reviews, and prioritize safety, authenticity, and value. They book directly or through trusted agents.
- Secondary persona: Honeymoon couples, family groups, solo adventurers, and corporate retreat planners
- Search intent profile:
  - 60% Informational (researching Tanzania travel: "best time to visit Tanzania", "what to see in Serengeti")
  - 30% Commercial (comparing operators: "best Tanzania safari companies", "Tanzania tour operators reviews")
  - 10% Transactional (ready to book: "book Tanzania safari", "Tanzania safari packages 2025")
