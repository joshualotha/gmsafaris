Safari Overview

Embark on a thrilling 12-day journey that takes you through the stunning landscapes of Tanzania's northern safari circuit and the pristine beaches of Zanzibar. This trip offers a mix of wildlife safaris, cultural experiences, and beach relaxation, ensuring an unforgettable adventure. From the serene Materuni Waterfalls and hunter-gatherer tribes to the iconic Serengeti National Park and the sparkling shores of Zanzibar, this tour is a perfect blend of nature, culture, and adventure.

Detailed Itinerary (13 Days including Arrival/Departure)

Arr

Arrival Day: Arrival in Arusha

Arusha

Upon your arrival in Arusha, our driver will pick you up from the airport, depending on your arrival time, and take you to your hotel (Korona Villa/Safari Lodge or similar). After checking in, you'll have time to relax and enjoy dinner at the hotel before an overnight stay.

Accommodation: Njiro Legacy / Alco Apartments (or Korona Villa/Safari Lodge as per text)

1

Travel to Tarangire National Park

Tarangire NP, Mto wa Mbu

After a hearty breakfast, we will head to Tarangire National Park. Once inside the park, you will enjoy a thrilling game drive for a few hours. After the game drive, you'll stop for a delightful lunch break. Following lunch, we will continue the safari and exit the park in the late afternoon. We will then drive to Mto wa Mbu, where you'll stay at Motto Cottage for the night.

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

Accommodation: Mawemawe Manyara Lodge / Farm of Dreams (or Motto Cottage as per text)

2

Mto wa Mbu, Lake Manyara National Park

Lake Manyara NP, Mto wa Mbu

Enjoy breakfast at the lodge before heading to Lake Manyara National Park for a full-day game drive. After exploring the park, we'll have a lunch break by the hot spring picnic site. In the afternoon, you'll return to Mto wa Mbu for cultural tourism programs before enjoying dinner and an overnight stay at Motto Cottage.

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

Accommodation: Mawemawe Manyara Lodge / Farm of Dreams (or Motto Cottage as per text)

3

Mto wa Mbu to Lake Eyasi (Hadzabe & Datooga Tribes)

Lake Eyasi, Mto wa Mbu

Head to Lake Eyasi to meet the Hadzabe tribe for a hunting expedition. Lunch break. Afternoon with Hadzabe women gathering roots/fruits, then visit Datooga boma. Drive back to Mto wa Mbu.

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

Accommodation: Mawemawe Manyara Lodge / Farm of Dreams

4

Early Drive to Ngorongoro & Serengeti (Ndutu Area)

Ngorongoro Crater, Ndutu (Southern Serengeti)

Drive to Ngorongoro Crater, descend for a game drive. Lunch at Gnotobiotic picnic site. Exit crater, drive to Ndutu for overnight at a bush camp or Heritage Camp.

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

Accommodation: Tanzania Bush Camps / Heritage Camp, Ndutu

5

Serengeti Game Drive (Ndutu to Seronera)

Serengeti National Park (Seronera)

Game drive from Ndutu Gate towards Seronera (central Serengeti). Full day exploring plains & wildlife. Lunch box on the go. Head to tented lodge.

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch Box, Dinner

Accommodation: Moyo Tented Camp / Tukaone Camp

6

Full Day Serengeti Safari

Serengeti National Park

Morning and afternoon game drives in Serengeti. Witness Great Migration (seasonal). Lunch box. Optional hot air balloon ride (additional cost). Return to camp/lodge.

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch Box, Dinner

Accommodation: Moyo Tented Camp / Tukaone Camp

7

Serengeti to Zanzibar

Serengeti, Zanzibar (Stone Town)

Early morning game drive, breakfast, then flight from Serengeti to Zanzibar. Check into Tembo Hotel in Stone Town. Dinner. Evening stroll around Forodhani Garden and food market.

Meals: Breakfast, Dinner

Accommodation: Tembo Hotel, Stone Town

8

Zanzibar - Stone Town & Nakupenda Island

Nakupenda Island, Stone Town

Boat ride to Nakupenda Island for beach time. Optional lunch on island or back in Stone Town. Relax and explore.

Meals: Breakfast, (Optional Lunch)

Accommodation: Tembo Hotel, Stone Town

9

Zanzibar - Jozani Forest or Prison Island & Spice Tour

Jozani Forest/Prison Island, Spice Plantation, Stone Town

Choose Jozani Forest (red colobus monkeys) or Prison Island. Enjoy a spice tour. Lunch, then back to Stone Town.

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch

Accommodation: Tembo Hotel, Stone Town

10

Zanzibar - Nungwi Beach

Nungwi Beach

Head to Nungwi Beach. Relax on the beach or enjoy water sports. Meals provided on-site.

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

Accommodation: Beach Hotel, Nungwi

11

Zanzibar - Safari Blue Tour

Menai Bay Conservation Area (Safari Blue)

Embark on a Safari Blue tour: snorkeling, mangrove walks, dhow boat. Drinks, refreshments, snorkeling gear included.

Meals: Breakfast, Lunch (Seafood BBQ usually included in Safari Blue)

Accommodation: Beach Hotel, Nungwi

12

Zanzibar - Dolphin Tour at Mnemba Island & Departure

Mnemba Atoll, Airport

Dolphin Tour at Mnemba Island (swim/snorkel). Return to hotel. Enjoy beach time before transfer to airport for departure.

Meals: Breakfast

Important Notes

  • Accommodation types vary from lodges, tented camps to beach hotels.
  • Includes domestic flight Serengeti to Zanzibar.
  • Materuni Waterfalls tour likely on arrival day or adjusted.
  • Travel insurance and international flights are not included.
